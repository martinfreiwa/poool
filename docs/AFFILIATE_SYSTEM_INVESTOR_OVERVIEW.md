# POOOL Affiliate Partner Syndicate — Investor Overview

> **Document type:** Business overview for investors and stakeholders
> **Last updated:** April 2026

---

## What It Is

POOOL operates a performance-based affiliate program — the **Partner Syndicate** — that allows vetted financial creators, wealth managers, and established investor communities to earn commissions by referring new investors to the platform.

This is not an open referral link anyone can generate. It is a **curated, compliance-first partner channel** with identity verification, a mandatory knowledge exam, legal agreement signing, and a multi-step payout validation process before any money changes hands.

The program functions as a scalable, cost-efficient customer acquisition engine. POOOL only pays commissions after a referred investor completes a real, settled investment — there are no costs for clicks, signups, or unqualified traffic.

---

## The Four Partner Categories

During the application process, every affiliate identifies their primary distribution channel. This categorises the partner network into four groups:

### 1. Financial Newsletter / Substack Authors
Independent financial writers with paid or free subscriber lists. These partners typically reach high-intent investors who already consume financial content regularly. Examples include market analysis newsletters, real estate investment publications, and private wealth education blogs.

### 2. YouTube & Video Content Creators
Creators producing financial education, property investment, or passive income content. Video audiences tend to be larger and more broadly distributed, with conversion rates tied to the creator's credibility and editorial positioning.

### 3. X (Twitter) / Social Media Communities
Operators of investor-focused social communities, Twitter accounts, or discussion groups. These partners often have highly engaged, niche audiences with strong peer-influence dynamics — well-suited for promoting private-market investment opportunities.

### 4. Private Wealth Advisors & Advisory Networks
Licensed or unlicensed wealth professionals who maintain existing client or prospect relationships. This is the highest-value partner category, as referrals from trusted advisors tend to convert with higher average investment sizes. POOOL's compliance framework is especially important here, as these partners must not cross into regulated investment advice.

> **Note:** The partner's audience size is also collected at application: under 5,000, 5,000–50,000, or over 50,000. This informs POOOL's review and tier expectations but does not automatically determine approval.

## The Affiliate System — End to End

### Stage 1: Discovery (Promo Page)
Non-approved users who navigate to the affiliate section land on a **locked promotional page** — they cannot access the dashboard or any partner tools until they apply and are approved.

The promo page presents:
- The program's value proposition for partners
- A summary of the 8-tier commission structure
- The three partner qualification criteria (Relevant Audience, Compliant Distribution, Quality Referrals)
- An interactive earnings calculator
- An FAQ section covering the most common questions about payout timing, qualified referrals, and permitted marketing activities
- Two calls to action: **Request Partner Access** and **Are You Qualified?**

Platform statistics are shown publicly: $12M+ assets under management, 24 live properties, 8 commission tiers, 1.75% maximum commission rate.

---

### Stage 2: Application & Vetting (Onboarding Wizard)

The application is a five-step guided process with a progress bar. Each step must be completed in order.

**Step 1 — Partner Profile**
The applicant describes their distribution channel and audience:
- Primary traffic source (Newsletter, YouTube, Twitter, Wealth/Advisory, or Other)
- Audience size bracket
- Main website or social profile URL
- Phone number (for POOOL's review team to follow up if needed)

**Step 2 — Identity Verification**
The system checks the applicant's existing KYC status on the platform. If identity verification has not been completed, the applicant must complete it before continuing. No referral code is ever issued to an unverified identity.

**Step 3 — Tax Information**
The applicant provides their tax identification number and, optionally, their legal company name if they intend to invoice as a business entity. A notice is shown at this step explaining the 30-day holdback rule and that tax documentation will be required before any payout is released.

**Step 4 — Legal Agreements**
Five legal documents are presented as tabbed content. The applicant must read each document and individually check a separate acceptance box for each:

1. **Affiliate Terms & Conditions** — Program structure, activation requirements, enforcement, and termination conditions
2. **Affiliate Code of Conduct** — What the affiliate may and may not do (role is referral only, not advice or sales)
3. **Qualified Referral & Payout Policy** — Definition of a qualified referral, the holdback period, and clawback conditions
4. **Approved Marketing Materials Policy** — What materials may be used, what is prohibited, and how original content must be handled
5. **Affiliate Privacy Notice** — What data POOOL collects and how it is used

Every acceptance is logged on the server with the affiliate's identity, IP address, timestamp, and the document version accepted. This creates an immutable compliance record.

**Step 5 — Compliance Exam**
A mandatory five-question knowledge test. All five questions must be answered correctly — a single wrong answer fails the exam and triggers a 10-minute lockout. The exam is validated server-side; it cannot be bypassed by manipulating the application.

The five questions test understanding of the most critical rules:
1. Are affiliates allowed to provide investment advice? *(No — referral information only)*
2. Can affiliates guarantee returns or asset safety? *(Never)*
3. When is commission paid? *(After 30-day holdback, KYC, and tax clearance)*
4. Can affiliates accept or forward client funds? *(Absolutely forbidden)*
5. Is cold outreach via WhatsApp or Telegram permitted? *(No)*

After passing the exam, the application enters admin review. The applicant sees a confirmation screen with a timeline: Onboarding Complete → Admin Review (1–3 business days) → Account Activation. An email confirmation is sent.

---

### Stage 3: Admin Review & Activation

The POOOL compliance team reviews every application manually. The admin checks:
- That the applicant's KYC status is fully approved
- The traffic source and audience claims are plausible
- No red flags in the application data

If approved, the admin assigns a unique referral code to the partner and activates their account. An activation email is sent. If rejected, a rejection email is sent with a reason.

## The Partner Portal — Page by Page

### Page 1: Partner Dashboard (Main Hub)

This is the affiliate's primary working screen. It is divided into two columns.

**Top row — three KPI cards:**
- **Total Earnings** — Lifetime commission earned, with a sub-label showing how much has been paid out
- **Available to Payout** — Commissions cleared and ready for withdrawal; includes a "Request Payout" button that is only active when a balance is available above the $50 minimum
- **Under 30-Day Holdback** — Commissions earned but not yet cleared through the review window; shown as pending qualification

**Left column — two panels:**

*Referral Link Widget:*
The affiliate's unique referral link is displayed with a one-click copy button. Above the link, an **Advanced Tracking** section allows affiliates to add optional campaign parameters:
- **SubID** — A custom tag the affiliate sets to identify a specific campaign (e.g. "email_blast_may"). This lets them track which campaign drove which referrals.
- **UTM Source** — The platform or channel (e.g. "twitter", "newsletter"). This feeds into POOOL's attribution reporting.

Below the link, a QR code is automatically generated for offline or print use.

Also in this panel: an **S2S Postback Webhook** field where advanced affiliates can enter a URL from external tracking platforms (Voluum, RedTrack, etc.). When a commission event occurs, POOOL pings this URL automatically, enabling professional affiliate trackers to log conversions in real time.

*Referral Funnel:*
A visual four-step funnel showing the conversion path for this affiliate's referrals:
- **Clicks** → **Signups** → **Invested (In Holdback)** → **Qualified**

This gives affiliates a clear picture of where their audience is dropping off and how many referrals are progressing through the pipeline.

**Right column — three panels:**

*Tier Progression:*
Shows the affiliate's current tier name and commission rate, a progress bar across all 8 tiers, and markers for where each tier threshold falls. The bar updates as more referrals qualify.

*Recent Commissions:*
A live list of the most recent commission entries — each showing the referral and its status.

*SubID Tracking Report:*
A table breaking down performance by campaign tag. For each SubID, it shows: clicks, registrations, earned commissions, and pending commissions. This allows affiliates to compare campaign performance at a glance.

## The Commission Structure in Full

Affiliates start at Access tier on approval and advance automatically as their lifetime qualified referral count grows. The tier determines the commission rate applied to every investment made by their referred users.

| Tier | Qualified Referrals Required | Commission Rate |
|---|---|---|
| Access | Entry — no minimum | 0.50% |
| Bronze | 3+ | 0.60% |
| Silver | 10+ | 0.75% |
| Gold | 25+ | 0.90% |
| Platinum | 50+ | 1.10% |
| Diamond | 100+ | 1.30% |
| Elite | 200+ | 1.50% |
| Ambassador | 500+ (invitation only) | 1.75% |

**Qualified referral definition:** A referred user who (1) was validly attributed to the affiliate, (2) completed full identity verification, (3) made a first eligible paid investment, (4) passed the 30-day review window with no fraud flags, and (5) had no disqualifying issues on either party's account.

**What does not count:** Clicks, signups without investment, incomplete KYC, cancelled transactions, or any referral flagged for fraud.

**Commission model:** Single-level only. There are no downlines, no recruitment bonuses, and no multi-level structure. An affiliate earns only on the investors they personally refer.

## The 30-Day Holdback — How Money Flows

When a referred investor makes their first qualifying investment, POOOL creates a provisional commission entry. This commission sits in a holdback period for 30 days. During those 30 days:

1. The investment must remain active (not cancelled or refunded)
2. The referred investor's account must remain in good standing
3. No fraud flags can be raised on either party
4. The affiliate's KYC and tax documentation must be in order

After all conditions are met, the commission moves from provisional to payable.

Payouts are then triggered manually by a POOOL admin — not automatically. The admin reviews the affiliate's tax readiness, marks them cleared, and initiates a payout batch. The funds move from POOOL's treasury to the affiliate's cash wallet in a single locked transaction. A full audit trail is written at every step.

If a qualifying event is later reversed (investment cancelled, fraud discovered, policy breach found), POOOL can claw back a commission even after it has been paid.

## Compliance & Legal Safeguards

**Three layers of legal protection:**

**Layer 1 — Pre-activation agreements**
Every affiliate explicitly signs five documents individually. Each acceptance is logged with their identity, IP address, timestamp, and the document version signed. If policies are updated to a new version, affiliates must re-sign before payouts can continue.

**Layer 2 — Investor-side disclosures**
When a referred investor reaches the investment checkout, they see an expanded set of disclosures triggered by the fact that they arrived via an affiliate link. This includes an acknowledgement that the referring party may earn a commission. These disclosures are permanently logged.

**Layer 3 — Tax compliance**
Tax classification and identification are collected at onboarding and stored encrypted. No payout can be released by an admin until the affiliate's tax status has been explicitly reviewed and cleared. Changing tax details freezes payouts automatically until re-verification is complete.

## Fraud Prevention

| Protection | How It Works |
|---|---|
| No self-referral | An affiliate cannot refer their own account |
| Click fraud throttling | Repeated clicks from the same IP within a short window are ignored |
| Single attribution | A referred user can be attributed to only one affiliate, even across multiple referral links |
| 24-hour IP deduplication | Same IP + same referral code within 24 hours generates only one recorded click |
| Commission clawback | Admins can reverse a paid commission and recover the funds from the affiliate's wallet |
| Immediate suspension | Admins can suspend an affiliate instantly, freezing their link and blocking all new attributions |

Advanced fraud ring detection — identifying circular networks where affiliate A refers affiliate B and B refers A — is in development.

## Admin Control Panel

The POOOL operations team manages the entire program through a dedicated admin interface with four sections:

**Affiliate Applications Desk**
A review queue of all pending partner applications. Each application shows the applicant's profile data, traffic source, audience size, KYC status, and exam completion. Admins can approve (assigning a referral code and commission rate) or reject (with a reason that is emailed to the applicant). Active affiliate count is shown as a live KPI.

**Finance & Tax Release Board**
A table of all commissions that have cleared the 30-day holdback and are ready for payout, grouped by affiliate. Admins review each affiliate's tax classification, mark them as tax-ready, and trigger payout batches. Each payout is an atomic operation — it either fully completes or fully rolls back. Bulk actions allow multiple affiliates to be paid in a single operation. Every action generates an audit log entry.

**Compliance & Suspension Tools**
Admins can suspend any active affiliate immediately. Suspension sends an automated notification email and blocks all new referral attribution. A clawback function allows recovery of commissions from the affiliate's wallet in cases of fraud or policy breach.

**Fraud Monitoring**
A visualisation of referral networks to detect suspicious patterns — affiliates who appear to be referring each other, clusters of referrals from the same IP address, or abnormal referral velocity. (Advanced graph-based detection is on the near-term roadmap.)

## Summary

The POOOL Partner Syndicate is a fully operational, compliance-first affiliate acquisition channel. It enables POOOL to grow its investor base through four categories of trusted partners — financial newsletter authors, video creators, social community operators, and private wealth advisors — paying only for verified results, only after a 30-day validation window, and with full legal documentation and fraud protection at every step.
