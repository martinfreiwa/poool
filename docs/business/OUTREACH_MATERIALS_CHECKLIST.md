## INVESTOR ENGAGEMENT PATHWAY

### Stage 1: Initial Contact
- Email outreach with executive summary
- Expected response: 1-2 weeks
- Success metric: 30-35% response rate

### Stage 2: Introduction Call (30 minutes)
- Project overview (10 min)
- Power economics (10 min)
- Partnership structure (5 min)
- Q&A (5 min)
- Outcome: Move to Stage 3 or follow-up

### Stage 3: Detailed Discussion
- Full information memorandum provided
- Management team call scheduled
- Financial modeling discussion
- Timeline discussion
- Outcome: Move to Stage 4 or archive

### Stage 4: Investment Committee Review
- Committee presentation materials
- Final Q&A session
- Investment decision timeline (60-90 days typical)
- Partnership term negotiation
- Outcome: Term sheet or archive

### Stage 5: Deal Completion
- Legal/financial due diligence
- Final negotiation
- Commitment agreement
- Fund deployment timeline
