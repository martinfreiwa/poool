2. Program Nature and Core Rule

2.1 The Program is a controlled, single-level, referral-only affiliate program.

2.2 In Phase 1, Affiliates act only as limited referrers. Affiliates may use only:

  • general information
  • Approved Materials
  • their personal approved Referral Link or referral code
  • and official POOOL pages and materials, to introduce users to POOOL.

2.3 Affiliates must not provide personal investment advice, recommend specific assets, promise or imply returns or safety, conduct private cold outreach, negotiate, close, handle customer funds or payments, or otherwise act as advisers, brokers, sellers, closers, or transaction intermediaries.

2.4 The Program is not an MLM, network-marketing, or downline-based structure. No Affiliate may earn from another Affiliate’s activity, recruit for commission, or receive override compensation.

2.5 If an Affiliate goes beyond general information, approved materials, personal referral tools, and official POOOL sources, that conduct falls outside the intended Phase 1 model and may result in review, restriction, suspension, payout denial, clawback, or termination.

3. Definitions

For purposes of these Terms: “Affiliate” means a person or entity approved by POOOL to participate in the Program and activated under these Terms. “Affiliate Communication” means communication that uses a Referral Link, referral code, referral call to action, commission-related redirection, profile/story redirect, systematic promotional behavior, or other conduct that falls within the regulated affiliate channel under the Program rules. “Approved Materials” means only those texts, visuals, pages, templates, scripts, assets, or other materials expressly approved by POOOL for Affiliate use. “Approved Referral Tool” means a Referral Link, code, landing path, attribution mechanism, or similar tool issued or approved by POOOL. “Customer” means a user, prospective user, investor, or prospective investor interacting with POOOL. “Direct Customer” means a Customer not classified by POOOL as referral-sourced under POOOL’s attribution rules. “Disqualifying Event” means any event preventing a referral or commission from becoming or remaining qualified, including fraud, refund, chargeback, reversal, self-referral, related-party self-referral, referral-ring activity, policy breach, invalid attribution, incomplete KYC, or any other event specified by POOOL in the Program Documents. “Holdback Period” means the review period following a first eligible paid investment before any related commission may become payable. The launch default is intended to be 30 days unless otherwise approved in writing. “KYC” means the know-your-customer, identity, verification, and onboarding review steps required by POOOL. “Lookback Period” means the rolling period used to assess Tier unlock, currently intended to be 12 months unless otherwise approved in writing. “Phase 1” means the first launch phase of the Program as a controlled referral-only model. “Private Conversation” means a genuinely personal, non-systematic conversation that does not include referral tools, referral CTA, commission-related redirection, or advisory/sales conduct. “Program Documents” means these Terms and the other policies, notices, acknowledgements, and operational rules incorporated into the Program. “Qualified Referral” means a referral satisfying all qualification conditions defined by POOOL, including valid attribution, completed KYC, completed first eligible paid investment, holdback/review clearance, and absence of any Disqualifying Event. “Referral Customer” means a Customer classified by POOOL as entering POOOL through a referral path under POOOL’s attribution logic. “Referral Link” means the Affiliate’s personal approved referral link or equivalent attribution tool. “Referral Ring” means a reciprocal, circular, coordinated, or manipulated referral structure intended to create improper commission eligibility, tier credit, or payout rights. “Single-Level” means a program structure in which Affiliates may earn only from their own qualified direct referrals and not from recruited or indirect network activity. “Tier” means an Affiliate status level under the Program’s 8-tier commission ladder.

. Affiliate Communication

8.1 Normal Private Conversations are not automatically treated as Affiliate Communication.
