Location

Cabai Rawit Plantation

Located in Lebak Regency, Banten Province, this 60-hectare chili plantation benefits from ideal tropical growing conditions and strategic proximity to major distribution hubs. The region's volcanic soil, consistent rainfall patterns, and favorable climate make it one of Indonesia's premier agricultural zones for high-yield chili cultivation. With direct access to Jakarta metropolitan area (approximately 150km) and Serang-Panimbang highway infrastructure, the plantation ensures efficient logistics for both raw and processed products. The area is home to established agricultural communities with deep expertise in chili farming, providing reliable labor and local knowledge. Lebak Regency's agricultural sector is supported by government programs and modern farming infrastructure, making it an ideal location for commercial-scale commodity production that serves both domestic and export markets.

Frequently asked questions

Can I change my investment amount later?

Yes, you can modify your investment amount before the property is fully funded. However, once funding is complete, changes are not possible as the investment is locked in.

What is the withdrawal policy?

You can withdraw your investment after the minimum holding period of 12 months. Early withdrawals may be subject to penalties and market conditions.

How are rental payments distributed?

Rental income is distributed monthly to all investors proportionally based on their ownership percentage. Payments are typically processed within the first week of each month.

What are the associated fees?

There's a 5% platform fee on investment amounts and up to 10% management fee on rental income. No hidden charges or additional fees apply.

What happens if the property value decreases?

Real estate investments carry market risk. While we carefully select properties in stable markets, property values can fluctuate. However, you'll continue to receive rental income regardless of short-term value changes.
