Operational & Sales Strategy

Contract farming & secured off-take

The plantation operates under pre-agreed purchase contracts with major buyers (Heinz ABC, Indofood, Sasa), ensuring predictable cash flow and reduced market risk.

Sustainable high-yield cultivation

Application of Nexbio bio-fertilizers and organic soil enhancers increases productivity by 30-50% while improving long-term land fertility.

Efficient logistics & processing

Harvest is processed and stored in cooled warehouses in Banten, supporting product consistency and minimizing post-harvest loss.
