About the Operator

PT. NEO AGRO SOLUTIONS is a full-cycle agribusiness operator (planting, agro-technologies, care, harvesting, processing, and distribution). The company is managed by two key figures who bring together political influence, technological leadership, and a solid resource base for the project.

Devi Erna Rachmawati

  • - Co-owner & Director of PT. NEO AGRO SOLUTIONS.
  • - Vice Chair of Agriculture at KADIN (Indonesian Chamber of Commerce and Industry).
  • - Founder of Nexbio Tech, supplier of bio-fertilizers and biostimulants increasing yields by 30-50%.
  • - Direct access to key industry players: food manufacturers (Heinz ABC, Indofood, Sasa), exporters (Japan, Korea, Singapore), and government agricultural programs.
  • - Through her role at KADIN and proximity to government, Devi ensures political support, strategic contracts with premium buyers, and project protection.

Mulyadi Jayabaya

  • - Co-owner of PT. NEO AGRO SOLUTIONS and landowner hosting the project.
  • - Prominent entrepreneur and politician, former Regent of Lebak Regency (Banten Province).
  • - One of the region's key business leaders, associated with multiple strategic projects in agriculture and construction.
  • - Provides 60 ha of land for the project, ensuring the legal and resource base for long-term operations.
