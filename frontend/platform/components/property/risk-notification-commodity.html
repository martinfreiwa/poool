Risk notification

While we strive to deliver the best for our customers, it's important to be aware of certain risks of full or partial loss of your investment that may be beyond our control:

Crop & Weather Risk:

Natural events such as drought, flooding, or pest outbreaks can reduce crop yields and impact returns.

Market Price Fluctuation:

Commodity prices are subject to global supply and demand dynamics, which may affect the final sale value of harvested produce.

Investment Duration:

Investments are locked until the harvest cycle is complete and produce is sold. Early withdrawal is not possible.
