Frequently asked questions

What is the minimum investment amount?

The minimum investment varies per commodity offering and is displayed on the asset page. You can invest any amount above the minimum in increments of one token.

How do I receive my returns?

Returns are distributed to your POOOL wallet after the harvest cycle is complete and the produce has been sold to contracted buyers. You'll receive notifications at each milestone.

Can I withdraw my investment early?

Commodity investments are locked for the duration of the farming cycle. Once the harvest is complete and sold, your returns including the ROI will be credited to your wallet.

What are the associated fees?

A platform fee is charged on your investment amount at checkout. There are no hidden charges or additional management fees. The fee percentage is displayed during checkout.

What happens if the crop fails?

All commodity investments carry agricultural risk. However, crops are insured against weather and pest damage, and the operator uses diversified planting strategies to minimize total loss exposure.
