Currency Usage Disclosure

All prices displayed in USD on this website are for international reference only. As required by Indonesian Law (PBI 17/3/PBI/2015 and Currency Law UU No. 7/2011), all transactions conducted in the territory of the Republic of Indonesia must be settled in Indonesian Rupiah (IDR).

Key Terms

  • USD prices are provided for user convenience and do not represent the final transaction currency.
  • All payments, legal agreements, invoices, and settlements are issued in Rupiah (IDR).
  • Currency conversions may apply depending on your payment method or gateway.
  • For transactions classified as international trade (cross-border services), payments in foreign currencies may be accepted within the scope of applicable laws.

By using this platform, you acknowledge and agree to the currency regulations of Indonesia.

A Word of Caution: POOOL does not offer financial, legal, tax, or investment advice. The information provided on this platform is for general informational purposes only.

Info, Documents and Support: For official documents, investment policies, and support, please refer to our Legal Center. Directorate General of Consumer Protection and Orderly Commerce Ministry of Trade of the Republic of Indonesia.
