Everything you need to know about the product

Do I really own something?+
Yes — every share you buy is backed by a digital ownership certificate notarised by an Indonesian Public Notary. Your ownership is legally binding under Indonesian law, recorded on an encrypted ledger, and fully transferable on our secondary marketplace.
Can I invest remotely?+
Absolutely. POOOL is fully online — sign up, complete KYC, fund your wallet, and buy shares from anywhere in the world. We currently have investors in 40+ countries.
How do I earn?+
You earn passive income through monthly rental yields distributed directly to your POOOL wallet. You also benefit from capital appreciation during the asset's holding period or when you exit your position on our secondary marketplace.
How can I exit?+
List your shares on our built-in peer-to-peer secondary marketplace anytime. There are no contractual lockups. Sale speed depends on demand — popular properties typically clear in hours to days.
Is this legal and regulated?+
POOOL is operated by PT. POOOL International Group, a foreign-owned (PMA) company registered in Indonesia and a subsidiary of our Hong Kong holding entity. All property ownership is notarised by a licensed Indonesian Public Notary. Transactions settle in IDR per Bank Indonesia regulations (PBI No. 17/2015 and Currency Law No. 7/2011).
What if the property doesn't perform?+
All projected yields are illustrative and not guaranteed. Real-world performance can vary with occupancy, seasonality, and market conditions. POOOL publishes monthly performance for every property so you can see exactly how your assets are performing — and exit anytime via the marketplace.
What about taxes?+
Tax treatment depends on your country of residence. POOOL provides annual income statements summarising your earnings and capital gains in your dashboard. We recommend consulting a local tax advisor for your specific situation.
