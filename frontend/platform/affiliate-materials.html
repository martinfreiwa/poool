Say / Do-Not-Say Reference

Quick compliance reference for affiliate communications. Use the left column wording. Avoid the right column under all circumstances.

Say
  • "POOOL is a fractional real-world asset platform — review the official information here: [your link]"
  • "You can find official fees, risks, and asset details on POOOL's pages."
  • "I'm sharing my referral link if you want to explore POOOL through the official site."
  • "Investment decisions are yours — POOOL's official materials are the right place to start."
  • "Here's where to find the risk disclosure: [official POOOL page]"
Do Not Say
  • "This is guaranteed / safe / risk-free."
  • "You should buy this villa / start with this asset."
  • "You will definitely make X% return."
  • "Send me the funds and I'll arrange it."
  • "DM me and I'll help you choose the right one."
  • "I can get you special terms / a private bonus / faster processing."
  • "This is the best investment / lowest-risk listing."

Default rule: if it sounds like advice, a sales pitch, a guarantee, or a private deal — do not say it. Redirect to official POOOL information instead.

Allowed Examples by Channel

Reference patterns for compliant referral content across the channels you may use.

Public posts

"If you want to see how POOOL works, you can review the official pages, fees, and risk information here: [referral link]"

Format: general description · approved visuals · official links · no specific asset rec · no return claim.

Videos / Reels

"Here is a platform I'm following — if you want to review the official information yourself, here is my referral link."

Format: informational · high-level · no "best one to buy" · no "you should invest now" · no personalized recommendations.

Stories

"Want to check out POOOL? Here's my referral link." · "Official info, fees, and risk details on the platform here."

Format: short CTA to review official info · referral sticker / swipe-up if approved · no pressure language · no advice.

Blog / Long-form

"An overview of POOOL's platform features, citing official POOOL pages for product, fee, and risk information."

Format: factual · cites/links official POOOL pages · no personalized recommendation · no investment conclusion · no unapproved forecasts.

Profile bio / link in bio

Approved referral link in profile bio · approved landing page · post caption · story link.

Format: own approved referral link only · directs to approved official POOOL pages · not paired with prohibited advisory or closing behavior.

Private chat / DM

Inbound interest from a friend asking what POOOL is — answer factually, then share your referral link only. No cold outreach. No follow-up to push commitment.

⚠ Caution: private cold outreach is prohibited. Repeated follow-up messaging counts as systematic promotion and falls under affiliate rules.
