Frequently Asked Questions

Navigate to Settings → KYC Verification or use the KYC link in your sidebar. You'll need a valid government-issued ID (passport, national ID, or driving license) and a selfie photo. The verification process typically takes 24–48 hours.

Go to Settings → Security to update your password or email address. For email changes, you'll need to verify the new address via a confirmation link.

Browse the Marketplace, select an asset, choose the number of tokens you want, and add them to your cart. Proceed to checkout to complete the purchase. Tokens are allocated instantly from your wallet balance.

Dividends are distributed based on the net income of each asset, proportional to your token holdings. Payouts are credited directly to your wallet. Check the Portfolio page for expected payout dates.

Go to your Wallet page and click "Deposit". You can deposit via bank transfer (USD or IDR) or card payment. Card payments are processed instantly; bank transfers take 1-3 business days.

Go to your Wallet page and click "Withdraw". Enter the amount and select your withdrawal method. Withdrawals are processed within 1-3 business days after admin approval.

Go to Settings → Security → Enable 2FA. Scan the QR code with an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy) and enter the verification code to activate.

If you suspect unauthorised access: 1) Change your password immediately via Settings → Security. 2) Submit an Urgent support ticket. 3) Review your recent transactions in the Wallet.
