Understanding Fractional Ownership

Fractional real estate investing allows multiple investors to collectively own a property through digital shares or tokens. Unlike traditional real estate, which requires hundreds of thousands of dollars and full ownership commitment, fractional platforms like POOOL let you invest from as little as $50.

"Real estate is the safest investment in the world — and with tokenization, it's finally accessible to everyone." — Martin Fischer, Co-founder of POOOL

Here's what makes fractional ownership so compelling for modern investors:

  • Lower capital requirements — Start with as little as $50 instead of $500,000+
  • Instant diversification — Spread risk across multiple properties, cities, and asset classes
  • Professional management — Expert property managers handle all operations
  • Regular income — Receive proportional rental income directly to your wallet
  • Transparency — All ownership records verified on the blockchain

How Tokenization Works

When a property is listed on POOOL, it is valued by independent certified appraisers. The total value is then divided into digital tokens, each representing a fixed ownership percentage. Investors can buy, hold, and eventually sell these tokens as easy as trading stocks.

  1. Property is professionally appraised and legally structured
  2. Tokens are minted on the blockchain representing ownership shares
  3. Investors purchase tokens through the POOOL marketplace
  4. Rental income is distributed monthly proportional to token holdings
  5. Token holders can vote on major property decisions

Legal Framework

All properties on POOOL are structured through legally registered special purpose vehicles (SPVs), ensuring your investment is backed by real legal ownership rights — not just a promise.

Getting Started on POOOL

Starting your fractional real estate journey takes less than 10 minutes. Here's the step-by-step process:

Visit poool.finance and sign up with your email. We support Google and Facebook login for convenience. The signup process is completely free.

Risk Management & Diversification

Even the most promising investments carry risk. Here's how to manage yours effectively:

Never put more than 20% of your investment portfolio into a single asset class. Real estate, even fractional, should be one piece of a diversified strategy.

Our recommended approach for new investors:

  • Start small — invest $50–200 across 3–5 different properties to learn the platform
  • Reinvest rental income — let compounding do the heavy lifting over time
  • Diversify geographically — mix Bali villas with urban apartments in different cities
  • Read all property documentation before investing — due diligence is still your responsibility

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my investment protected if POOOL goes out of business?

Yes. Each property is held in an independent SPV (Special Purpose Vehicle) that is separate from POOOL's operational company. If POOOL ceased operations, a trustee would take over management and your ownership would remain intact.

When do I receive rental income?

Rental income is distributed monthly, directly to your POOOL wallet balance. You can reinvest it immediately or withdraw to your bank account. Distributions typically occur on the 1st–5th of each month for the prior month's rent.

Can I sell my tokens before the holding period ends?

Yes. POOOL's secondary market allows token-to-token trading between investors. Liquidity depends on market demand, but prime properties typically see active trading within 24–48 hours of listing.
