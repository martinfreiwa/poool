Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the POOOL Partner Syndicate.

What are POOOL affiliates allowed to do?

Approved POOOL affiliates may:

  • share general information about POOOL,
  • use approved marketing materials,
  • share their personal referral link,
  • and direct users to official POOOL pages, documents, fees, and risk information.

Affiliates may create public content such as posts, videos, stories, and blogs, as long as the content stays factual, uses approved materials, and does not cross into personal advice or sales activity.

Affiliates are not allowed to recommend specific assets, promise returns, negotiate special terms, handle payments, or act as personal investment advisers.

Investment decisions must always be made independently based on official POOOL information.

When in doubt: share official POOOL information and your referral link only. Do not answer in a way that sounds like advice or a sales pitch.

What documents govern the POOOL Affiliate Program?
The core affiliate-facing documents are the Affiliate Terms & Conditions, Affiliate Code of Conduct, Approved Marketing Materials Policy, Qualified Referral & Payout Policy, and Affiliate Privacy Notice. You'll review all five during onboarding. Tax and complaint procedures are covered in the Affiliate Tax Policy and Complaints & Investigations pages.
How does the commission structure work?
POOOL uses a single-level, performance-based model with 8 tiers. Your commission rate (0.50%–4.50%) is determined by your qualified referral volume over a rolling 12-month window after backend validation. There are no downlines, no recruitment bonuses, and no MLM structure.
When do I get paid?
Commission becomes payable only after a referral is fully qualified: valid attribution, completed KYC, first eligible paid investment, 30-day review period cleared, no unresolved issues, and your payout/tax setup is complete. Payouts are processed after all checks pass.
What is a Qualified Referral?
A Qualified Referral is a referred user who is validly attributed to you, completes onboarding and KYC, makes a first eligible paid investment, clears the review period, and passes all final payout checks. A click, signup, or incomplete KYC does not create a payable commission.
Can I create my own marketing materials?
You must use POOOL-approved marketing materials. If you create original content (blog posts, videos, podcasts) that references POOOL, it must stay factual, non-advisory, and direct users to official POOOL information. Custom pitch decks, modified brand assets, and guaranteed-return claims are strictly prohibited.
How long does the application review take?
Applications are typically reviewed within 1–3 business days. After completing the 5-step onboarding wizard (profile, identity, tax setup, legal agreements, compliance exam), your application enters admin review. You'll receive an email notification once approved.
