Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the POOOL Partner Syndicate.

What documents govern the POOOL Affiliate Program?
The core affiliate-facing documents are the Affiliate Terms & Conditions, Affiliate Code of Conduct, Approved Marketing Materials Policy, Qualified Referral & Payout Policy, and Affiliate Privacy Notice. You'll review all five during onboarding.
How does the commission structure work?
POOOL uses a single-level, performance-based model with 8 tiers. Your commission rate (0.50%–1.75%) is determined by qualified referral count after backend validation. There are no downlines, no recruitment bonuses, and no MLM structure.
When do I get paid?
Commission becomes payable only after a referral is fully qualified: valid attribution, completed KYC, first eligible paid investment, 30-day review period cleared, no unresolved issues, and your payout/tax setup is complete. Payouts are processed after all checks pass.
What is a Qualified Referral?
A Qualified Referral is a referred user who is validly attributed to you, completes onboarding and KYC, makes a first eligible paid investment, clears the review period, and passes all final payout checks. A click, signup, or incomplete KYC does not create a payable commission.
Can I create my own marketing materials?
You must use POOOL-approved marketing materials. If you create original content (blog posts, videos, podcasts) that references POOOL, it must stay factual, non-advisory, and direct users to official POOOL information. Custom pitch decks, modified brand assets, and guaranteed-return claims are strictly prohibited.
How long does the application review take?
Applications are typically reviewed within 1–3 business days. After completing the 5-step onboarding wizard (profile, identity, tax setup, legal agreements, compliance exam), your application enters admin review. You'll receive an email notification once approved.
