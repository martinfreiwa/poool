Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about fractional investing with POOOL.

What exactly is POOOL?
POOOL is a fractional real estate and commodity investment platform that enables anyone to invest in fully-vetted, high-yield assets starting from just $15. We handle all operations, legalities, and tenant management.
How do I earn money?
You earn passive income through monthly rental yields distributed directly to your POOOL wallet. Additionally, you benefit from capital appreciation during the asset's holding period or when you exit your position on the secondary market.
Are the investments legally binding and secure?
Yes, absolutely. Every asset on POOOL is fully notarized by an Indonesian Public Notary. Your ownership is legally bound, compliant with local jurisdictions, and documented on an encrypted ledger within our platform.
When can I sell my shares?
You can list your shares instantly on our built-in peer-to-peer secondary market anytime you choose. We ensure liquidity without the typical real estate lockups—you are always in control of your exit.
Do I need to be an accredited investor?
No. POOOL believes in total financial inclusion. We have removed the minimum investment barriers typically reserved exclusively for high-net-worth individuals, allowing anyone to start building wealth immediately.
