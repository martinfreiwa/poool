## KEY MESSAGING PRINCIPLES

1. **Formality Matches Investor Type:**
   - Royal FOs: Highly formal, government-aligned language
   - Network Hubs: Professional but streamlined
   - Emerging FOs: Direct, relationship-focused
   - Secondary Funds: Standard business development tone

2. **Value Proposition is Clear:**
   - Power economics always emphasized
   - Infrastructure-class security noted
   - Digital asset exposure highlighted
   - Limited partnership positioning stressed

3. **Call-to-Action is Direct:**
   - Always ask for next step
   - Provide specific options
   - Include contact information
   - Respect decision-making timeline

4. **Timing Acknowledgment:**
   - Reference institutional decision cycles
   - Offer flexibility in scheduling
   - Provide pre-meeting materials
   - Follow up appropriately
