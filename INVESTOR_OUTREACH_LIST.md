## Outreach Strategy

### Phase 1: Tier 1 & 2 VCs (Weeks 1-3)
**Target:** Traditional VC firms and blockchain-specialized funds  
**Approach:**
- Personalized email introduction emphasizing RWA market opportunity
- 1-pager with POOOL differentiators
- Request 15-20 minute intro call
- Track engagement and follow-up timing

### Phase 2: Strategic Corporate Partners (Weeks 3-6)
**Target:** Traditional finance institutions and fintech platforms  
**Approach:**
- Emphasize partnership opportunities and revenue-sharing models
- Highlight compliance and institutional-grade security
- Focus on strategic value beyond capital
- Include integration possibilities

### Phase 3: Angel Investors & Ecosystem (Weeks 6-10)
**Target:** Crypto-native angels and platform partnerships  
**Approach:**
- Leverage existing relationships in crypto community
- Offer early-investor perks or token allocation (if applicable)
- Present as emerging infrastructure opportunity
- Request intros to institutional contacts
