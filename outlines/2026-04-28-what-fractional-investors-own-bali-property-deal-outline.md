### H2: Do Fractional Investors Own The Actual Bali Property? (~330 words)

- Answer-first opener: not in the same way a direct buyer owns or controls a property; the structure and documents define the investor's rights.
- Key points:
  - Direct title/lease rights are different from fractional investor rights.
  - Bali foreign-ownership rules make wording especially important.
  - Economic exposure can be useful without being personal land ownership.
- H3: Title rights vs contract rights vs economic rights
  - Define each in one short paragraph.
- Key statistic/source to find: counsel-approved summary of Indonesian land-right rules from UU No. 5/1960 and PP No. 18/2021.
- Chart suggestion: matrix comparing title, contract, economic, and platform-record rights.
- Image placement: No.

### H2: What Role Does The SPV Play In A Fractional Property Deal? (~340 words)

- Answer-first opener: an SPV is the project structure that can separate one property deal from the platform's broader business, but the legal effect depends on the documents.
- Key points:
  - Why projects use SPVs.
  - What the SPV may hold or control.
  - What the investor agreement may reference.
  - What SPVs do not automatically guarantee.
- H3: Why asset isolation is different from risk elimination
  - Explain that isolation can limit cross-project confusion but does not remove property, operator, legal, or liquidity risk.
- Key statistic/source to find: POOOL product/legal source of truth for actual SPV role.

### H2: What Are Economic Rights In Plain English? (~330 words)

- Answer-first opener: economic rights are contract-defined rights to specified financial outcomes, such as a share of net distributions if available.
- Key points:
  - Rental income participation is not the same as guaranteed passive income.
  - Deductions, reserves, taxes, fees, and operating costs matter.
  - Sale proceeds, appreciation, or exit proceeds should be discussed only if documents allow.
- H3: Gross revenue is not investor distribution
  - Connect to ROI draft and gross-to-net waterfall.
- Key statistic/source to find: approved POOOL fee/distribution policy plus BPS tourism context only as market context.
- Chart suggestion: waterfall table from gross property revenue to investor distribution.
