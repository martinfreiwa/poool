## Compliance And Fact-Check Risks

- Do not say or imply foreign individuals can personally own Hak Milik/freehold land.
- Do not call any structure the safest, guaranteed, protected, or risk-free option.
- Do not repeat POOOL SPV, ERSA, escrow, NFT/certificate, investor-rights, or regulated-language claims without internal legal/product approval.
- Do not use BPS tourism arrivals or hotel room occupancy as proof of villa-level occupancy, rental yield, or investor return.
- Do not merge gross yield, net yield, cash-on-cash return, IRR, capital appreciation, and investor distributions into one "ROI" number.
- Do not promise secondary liquidity, buybacks, resale price, floor pricing, or fast exits.
